Tourist Information & Contact

Information

To make the most of your holiday in Ireland, make sure your first stop is a visit to one of the tourist information centres where you will receive free and independent advice, information, maps and literature to enhance your holiday experience.

Cork Tourist Information Centre

125 St. Patrick's Street, Cork 1850 230 330 corktio@failteireland.ie

The friendly and professional staff are happy to help you discover the many places of interest around the country, including Ireland's Ancient East, the Wild Atlantic Way, Ireland's Hidden Heartlands, Dublin and its surrounding areas.

The information centre provides:

  • Itinerary planning, local and national information
  • Free map and guide
  • Booking service for attractions, events, festivals, tours and more.
